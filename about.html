ABOUT US

Welcome to the Clown School! We are a community of passionate individuals dedicated to the art of clowning. Our school was founded in 2005 by the legendary clown, Chuckles the Magnificent. Chuckles spent his entire life entertaining audiences all over the world with his unique brand of humor and joy. Before his passing, Chuckles wanted to pass on his knowledge and love of clowning to future generations, and thus, the Clown School was born.
At our school, we believe that laughter is the best medicine, and that clowning is a powerful tool for spreading joy and positivity. Our faculty is comprised of professional clowns with decades of experience, who are dedicated to sharing their knowledge and skills with our students.
We pride ourselves on providing a safe and inclusive environment for all of our students, regardless of age, race, gender, or experience level. Our goal is to help each student discover their own unique clown persona and style, and to empower them to share their talents with the world.
Here at the Clown School, we believe that clowning is not just about making people laugh. It's also about connecting with others, spreading happiness, and creating memorable experiences. We take our mission very seriously, and we are proud to have produced some of the most talented and beloved clowns in the world. Thank you for considering the Clown School for your clowning education. We look forward to sharing our passion for the art of clowning with you!

OUR FACILITIES

Makeup Room

Our makeup room is fully stocked with professional-grade makeup and supplies, including face paint, brushes, and sponges. Students can experiment with different clown makeup looks and techniques, or receive guidance and feedback from our experienced makeup artists.

Performance Space

We have a large performance space that is used for student showcases and special events. The space is equipped with a stage, sound system, and lighting, allowing students to experience what it's like to perform in front of a live audience.

Lounge Area

Our lounge area is the perfect place for students to relax and socialize between classes. The area features comfortable seating, a TV, and a small kitchenette with a refrigerator, microwave, and coffee maker.
